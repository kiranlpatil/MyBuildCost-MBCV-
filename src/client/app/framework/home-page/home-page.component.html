Your Construction Cost

What is the estimated project cost?

This is the first question from the management that comes at the time of starting a new project. Answering this incorrectly at the starting phase will surely land to cost overruns and time delays. Any successful project needs well derived quantity and cost estimation. My Build Cost does exactly the same!

No obligation. No card details required.

Features

My Build Cost is the only Pre-construction platform to budget & estimate your project cost. It is a solid tool to control your project cost within your budget. Now with My Build Cost available as SaaS (Software as a Service), it’s time to say good bye to complicated spreadsheets.

Inbuilt technical data | No extensive data entry is required

My Build Cost is not just a software platform. It has in built Rate Analysis which has been verified on various projects. My Build Cost is a ready to use platform where all cost heads, construction items are already listed along with Rate analysis. You just have to select them & edit if required.

Easy to use, simple, yet powerful

My Build Cost is very user friendly and self explanatory platform. No special training is required. But still if you have any queries, we have provided demo videos for self learning. We also provide online support at no extra cost.

Generates budget at a click!

You just need to enter few project details and My Build Cost provides you the budget divided into around 80 cost heads.You can then edit & freeze it as per your project location and economics.

Matches your estimate with the budget

Once your budget is frozen, My Build Cost allows you to do reverse engineering & match your estimate with the budget.

Controls the project cost efficiently

Once your budget is frozen and estimate is matched with the budget, you just need to refer and follow My Build Cost and its reports while purchasing and contract allotting to keep your project cost within the budget.

Access it online from anywhere

My Build Cost is a cloud based web application. You can access it from anywhere on any computer.

Detailed yet simple to read reports

My Build Cost provides various reports about budgeted cost, estimated cost and material take off. Reports are generated automatically at the back end which you can download. These reports are prefect guide to your purchase, contracts and estimation team to control the project cost within the budget.

for estimation?

    project cost in around 80 cost heads & set the budget for each cost head to achieve your targeted cost. Then
    we do reverse engineering to match the actual estimate with budget by selecting rightly priced materials.
  • Technology advantage for cost estimation: Our competitors still use complex Excel spreadsheets
    which are difficult to use for controlling the project cost and you end up using them just to check the quantities. We provide the estimation in our self developed Web app “ My Build Cost” at no extra cost. Using My Build Cost you can easily monitor and control the construction cost throughout the project duration.
  • Technology advantage for material take off: We use international tools for quantity take off to get reliable and accurate quantities. Much lesser than our competitors!
  • Construction know how: Our directors and technical team have vast experience of on site construction. We have knowledge about latest construction practises and ongoing material/ labour rates. Our Rate analysis is based on practical on site data. All of these enables us to provide more accurate cost estimates than our competitors.

Few reasons for why you should
outsource the cost estimation

In house estimation

  • Third party auditing for Cost estimation is important as in case of material consumption variation or cost escalations, site technical team and estimation team end up in either making adjustments or arguments without any solutions.
  • Your in house estimation team remains busy with the estimation etc and is less focused on the cost control which is more important.
  • Many times the Estimations which are done in house are poorly documented in complicated Excel sheets. They miss some of the cost heads, have incorrect Rate analysis/ Quantity take offs, have issues about duplication of files etc. Its very difficult to control the Project cost using them. Many times its not reported to the Management level. And you end up getting Cost surprises towards the project completion.
  • Having more experienced estimation team and lesser projects can sometimes prove more costlier than outsourcing.
  • Job changes in the in house estimation team during project execution creates lot of problems for tracking of quantity and cost which results in cost escalation.

Outsourced estimation to My Build Cost

  • Outsourcing provides third party auditing of the Cost estimates and you can be most assured. We can easily assess and resolve the conflicts about excess material consumption and cost escalation if any at early stages so that the targeted project cost is not exceeded.
  • Your in house estimation team can focus on Cost control during the project duration by referring our estimates. In house team can guide the Purchase and Contracts team and approve the PO’s/WO’s to achieve the targeted project cost perfectly.
  • Our estimates are well stored on Cloud in My Build Cost. It has in built rate analysis and material take off. It creates various reports at the back end. It remains at one place. No issue about duplication of Excel files/ folders which creates confusion amongst teams.
  • We have core expertise for estimation and can manage the operational costs better because of larger volume of work and in turn prove more cost effective.
  • Our estimation expertise work in teams so that responsibility of quantity and cost estimation is perfectly carried through out the project duration.
